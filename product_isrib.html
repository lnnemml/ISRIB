The Science behind ISRIB

🔬 Discovery at UCSF

ISRIB was reported by Peter Walter’s group at UCSF to restore learning & memory in brain‑injured and elderly mice by modulating the Integrated Stress Response via eIF2B.

🧪

Published Research

Featured in peer‑reviewed journals (Science, eLife, Nature).

How ISRIB Works

1

ISR Activation

Stress triggers eIF2α phosphorylation and translation shut‑down.

2

eIF2B Modulation

ISRIB binds eIF2B to counteract the inhibition.

3

Cognitive Restoration

Protein synthesis resumes to support plasticity & memory.
