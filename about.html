Our Mission

ISRIB Shop was founded with a singular mission: to provide researchers worldwide with access to the highest quality research chemicals available. We believe that groundbreaking scientific discoveries require reliable, pure compounds that researchers can trust.

Since our establishment in 2020, we have been dedicated to supporting the global research community by maintaining the strictest quality standards and providing comprehensive documentation for every compound we supply.

Our Story

ISRIB Shop began when our founder, a former neuroscience researcher, experienced firsthand the challenges of sourcing reliable research chemicals. Too often, experiments were compromised by impure compounds or inconsistent quality from suppliers.

Recognizing the critical need for a trustworthy source of research chemicals, we set out to create a company that would prioritize quality above all else. We established partnerships with accredited laboratories and implemented rigorous testing protocols to ensure every compound meets the highest standards.

Today, ISRIB Shop serves researchers in over 50 countries, from individual investigators to major research institutions. Our commitment to quality and scientific integrity has made us a trusted partner in advancing human knowledge.
