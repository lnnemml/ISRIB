Our Mission

ISRIB Shop was founded with a singular mission: to provide researchers worldwide with access to the highest quality research chemicals through personalized service and direct communication. We believe that groundbreaking scientific discoveries require not only reliable, pure compounds, but also the support and guidance that comes from working directly with fellow scientists.

Since our establishment in 2020, we have been dedicated to supporting the global research community by maintaining the strictest quality standards while providing personalized consultation for every order. Our approach combines scientific rigor with human connection.

Our Story

ISRIB Shop began when our founder, a former neuroscience researcher, experienced firsthand the challenges of sourcing reliable research chemicals. Too often, experiments were compromised by impure compounds, inconsistent quality, or impersonal automated systems that couldn't provide the guidance researchers needed.

Recognizing the critical need for a trustworthy source that combined quality with personal service, we set out to create a company that would prioritize both scientific rigor and human connection. We established partnerships with accredited laboratories and implemented rigorous testing protocols, while maintaining the personal touch that large suppliers had abandoned.

Today, ISRIB Shop serves researchers in over 50 countries, from individual investigators to major research institutions. Our commitment to quality and personalized service has made us a trusted partner in advancing human knowledge, one conversation at a time.
