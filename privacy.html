Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how isrib.shop (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects personal data when you visit our website or contact us.

1) Data We Collect

  • Contact data: name, email, Telegram handle, and any message you submit via forms or chat.
  • Order-related data: product interest, pack/price selections sent via forms (for manual payment processing).
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device info, pages visited (via standard server logs and basic analytics if enabled).
  • Chat data: messages sent through our live chat widget (Tawk.to).

2) How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to inquiries and process manual orders.
  • To provide support and communicate about your request or shipment.
  • To maintain site security, prevent abuse, and improve site performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., record keeping).

3) Legal Bases (GDPR-style)

  • Contract/steps prior to contract: handling your request and order details.
  • Legitimate interests: running, improving, and securing our website and services.
  • Consent: where required (e.g., certain cookies/marketing, if enabled).
  • Legal obligation: where we must retain data by law.

4) Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, and then delete or anonymize it, unless a longer retention is required by law or for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims.

5) Sharing of Data

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., site hosting, form processing, live chat) strictly to operate the site and answer your requests.
  • Authorities when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

6) International Transfers

Our service providers may process data in other countries. Where applicable, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) to protect your information.

7) Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, data portability, or to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

8) Cookies & Analytics

We use only minimal cookies necessary to operate the site. If we enable additional analytics or marketing cookies in the future, we will update this Policy and, where required, request your consent.

9) Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. However, no online transmission or storage system is 100% secure.

10) Third‑Party Services

  • Live chat: Tawk.to — messages you send via the widget are processed by Tawk.to on our behalf.
  • Forms: If using a third‑party or static host processing (e.g., Netlify Forms), form submissions are handled by that provider.

11) Contact

For privacy requests or questions, please contact us via the contact page or Telegram (@your_handle). We will reply as soon as possible.

12) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated Policy.
